Holiday Events

Seasonal events run during each holiday activation window. Tasks and objectives below are synced to the latest server setup. Reward balancing is still being tuned by staff.

Activation and website visuals

  • Each holiday has a one-week activation window around its date.
  • The main homepage now shows a seasonal badge and lightweight themed effects during active holiday weeks.
  • Easter adds hidden eggs + a bunny animation on the homepage.

New Year — Midnight Meteor Relay

Objective: Hunt phantoms and collect gunpowder in the holiday zone.

Tasks: Kill 10 PHANTOM, collect 64 GUNPOWDER.

Rewards: 64 FIREWORK_ROCKET, 500 XP.

Valentine's Day — Cupid's Lost Letters

Objective: Collect roses and cocoa beans in the holiday zone.

Tasks: Break 24 ROSE_BUSH, collect 32 COCOA_BEANS.

Rewards: 8 GOLDEN_APPLE, 400 XP.

St. Patrick's Day — Emerald Gremlin Hunt

Objective: Kill cave spiders and gather emeralds from the event zone.

Tasks: Kill 20 CAVE_SPIDER, collect 16 EMERALD.

Rewards: 24 EMERALD, 450 XP.

Easter — Colossal Bunny Rampage

Objective: Defeat giant bunnies (rabbits) and gather eggs for the Spring Totem.

Tasks: Kill 30 RABBIT, collect 64 EGG.

Rewards: 1 TOTEM_OF_UNDYING, 700 XP.

Independence Day — Powder Keg Convoy

Objective: Collect TNT and gunpowder while defending the holiday zone.

Tasks: Collect 64 GUNPOWDER, collect 16 TNT.

Rewards: 16 TNT, 64 FIREWORK_ROCKET.

Halloween — Lanterns of the Hollow

Objective: Eliminate undead and carve pumpkins in the holiday zone.

Tasks: Kill 35 ZOMBIE, break 20 PUMPKIN.

Rewards: 16 JACK_O_LANTERN, 600 XP.

Thanksgiving — Harvest Guardian Feast

Objective: Gather harvest crops from the event area.

Tasks: Collect 64 WHEAT, 64 CARROT, 64 POTATO.

Rewards: 32 GOLDEN_CARROT, 500 XP.

Christmas — Snowman Salvage Operation

Objective: Recover snowman parts by collecting snow blocks and killing strays.

Tasks: Collect 64 SNOW_BLOCK, kill 20 STRAY.

Rewards: 6 DIAMOND, 650 XP.
