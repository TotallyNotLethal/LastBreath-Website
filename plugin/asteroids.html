Asteroid Events

Asteroids are timed world events that crash into configured worlds and broadcast their coordinates globally. The farther from spawn an asteroid lands, the higher its tier, mob pressure, and loot ceiling.

Spawn cadence

  • Random timer between 1200–3600s.
  • 5% chance to bias impact near players (up to 25,000 blocks).
  • 20% meteor shower chance on spawn cycle.
  • 10s countdown before impact.

Combat space rules

  • Impact blast radius: 2 blocks.
  • Mob leash radius: 16 blocks.
  • Base reaction radius: 32 blocks.
  • Per-mob reaction multipliers and minimum floors are configurable for pursuit pressure.

Tier system by distance

Tier Distance threshold Core mobs Default count Boss access
Tier 1 Base tier Zombie, Skeleton 4 mobs None
Tier 2 10,000+ blocks Zombie, Skeleton, Blaze, Magma Cube 6 mobs None
Tier 3 20,000+ blocks Zombie, Skeleton, Wither Skeleton, Vex, Piglin Brute 8 mobs Ravager / Warden bosses

Each tier can also apply potion effects and AOE zones (radius, duration, particles, delay) from config, with stronger defaults on higher tiers.

Tier 3 boss mechanics

Tier 3 bosses can roll advanced ability packages tracked internally by mechanic metadata keys. Expect zone denial, burst windows, and movement checks:

Projectile & area mechanics

  • Projectile blast detonations.
  • Firewall patterns that apply fire pressure.
  • Ravager slam impact shocks.

Boss-specific signatures

  • Warden pulse knockback/control windows.
  • Piglin Brute flame waves.
  • Wither nova with stacked team punishment.

Loot profile

Asteroid cores always drop scaled resource bundles, then roll optional rare drops, custom utility items, enchant pages, and (Tier 3 only) cosmetic aura tokens.

Guaranteed scaled materials

Iron, gold, emeralds, diamonds, and chance-based netherite scrap scale per tier with min/max config ranges.

Rare roll table

Totem of Undying, Enchanted Golden Apple, Nether Star, Ancient Debris, Dragon Breath, Heavy Core, and Dragon Egg with higher tier odds.

Special systems

Tier 2/3 can roll Rebirth Stone, Totem of Life, and Enhanced Grindstone. Enchant pages are configurable with extra rolls; Tier 3 guarantees at least one page if none hit.

Tier 3 also has a 2% default chance to drop one cosmetic aura token from the configured aura pool.
