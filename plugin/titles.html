LastBreath Titles

Titles are unlockable progression milestones. Equip one title at a time to activate its stat bonuses. This page shows exactly how to earn each title and the effects it grants.

How title effects work

  • Each title grants the listed passive bonuses while equipped.
  • Only your equipped title bonuses are active.
  • World Scaler is a special background-style title and can be toggled on/off.
