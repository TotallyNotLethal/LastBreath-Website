Custom Enchants

This page covers the player-facing custom enchants in LastBreath. No admin/config info, just what to use and what each enchant is for.

How Enchants Work

  • Apply enchant tomes in an anvil with the matching netherite gear.
  • Each item can hold multiple custom enchants when they are compatible.
  • To temporarily disable an enchanted item's custom effects, Shift + Right Click while holding it.
  • Guard enchants are exclusive: you can only have one of Wither Guard, Storm Guard, Lifesteal Ward, or Sonic Guard on the same armor piece.

Tool Enchants

NETHERITE PICKAXE

  • Auto Pickup — sends mined drops directly to your inventory.
  • Quarry — speeds up large area mining tasks.
  • Vein Miner — breaks connected ore veins more efficiently.
  • Smelter Touch — auto-smelts supported mined blocks.
  • Prospector — improves resource-finding gameplay while mining.

NETHERITE AXE

  • Auto Pickup — sends chopped drops directly to your inventory.
  • Tree Feller — chops whole trees much faster.

NETHERITE SHOVEL

  • Auto Pickup — sends dug materials directly to your inventory.
  • Excavator — clears dirt/sand/gravel areas faster.

NETHERITE HOE

  • Auto Pickup — sends harvested crops directly to your inventory.
  • Auto Replant — replants crops automatically during harvest loops.
  • Fertile Harvest — improves farming efficiency and yield flow.
  • Swift Tiller — speeds up preparing farmland.

Armor & Mobility Enchants

NETHERITE ARMOR (Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots)

  • Wither Guard — protection focused on wither-based threats.
  • Storm Guard — protection focused on storm/lightning pressure.
  • Lifesteal Ward — protection against lifesteal-style effects.
  • Sonic Guard — protection against sonic/shockwave damage sources.

Only one guard enchant can exist on a single armor piece at a time.

ELYTRA

  • Telegraph Null — utility enchant for safer/cleaner aerial movement situations.
