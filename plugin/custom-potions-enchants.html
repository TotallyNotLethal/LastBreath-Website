How Potions Are Made (Cauldron Brewing)

  1. Place a cauldron and fill it with water.
  2. Put the cauldron directly on top of a lit soulfire campfire.
  3. Use water bottles or base potions as your brew base.
  4. Add the potion's configured craftingIngredient to create that brew.
  • allowFromAwkward: true means the potion can be brewed from awkward potion routes.
  • allowFromAwkward: false means it is progression/branch gated.
  • scaryToDrink: true flags high-risk flavor/tier brews.
  • Glowstone Dust makes a potion stronger, Redstone makes it last longer, and Ghast Tears remove negative potion effects.
