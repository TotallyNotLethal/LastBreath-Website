LastBreath Plugin Overview

This page is the player-facing overview of what LastBreathHC adds to hardcore survival. It covers progression, events, teams, rewards, combat pressure, and quality-of-life systems. Admin-only tooling is intentionally left out.

1) Nemesis progression

The flagship loop. High-threat captains can emerge from normal hostile encounters and evolve over time.

  • Captains scale in strength with tier and level growth.
  • Traits, personalities, and memory systems shape future encounters.
  • Reinforcement behavior, social links, and rivalry pressure create recurring conflicts.
  • Rewards and progression tracking make long-term hunting meaningful.
Open detailed Nemesis guide →

2) World bosses

Large-scale raid encounters with rotating mechanics, anti-cheese rules, and special reward bundles.

  • Boss roster includes Gravewarden, StormHerald, HollowColossus, and AshenOracle.
  • Spawns can be timed or triggered by world conditions/events.
  • Drops can include materials, cosmetics, and title unlocks.
Open world boss breakdown →

3) Asteroid events

Server events where impact zones spawn combat waves and tiered loot opportunities.

  • Spawn windows and impact countdowns create contested objectives.
  • Higher tiers bring stronger hazards and better loot rolls.
  • Loot includes materials, custom item chances, and cosmetic outcomes.
Open asteroid event guide →

4) Holiday events

HolidayType-driven seasonal content runs around each holiday date with event-specific objectives, tasks, and rewards.

  • Includes New Year, Valentine's Day, St. Patrick's Day, Easter, Independence Day, Halloween, Thanksgiving, and Christmas.
  • Each event includes explicit objective text and tracked task counts (kill, collect, or break goals).
  • The website homepage now adds lightweight holiday-themed visuals during active holiday weeks.
Open holiday events wiki →

5) Blood Moon

A global pressure event that boosts danger and can interact with other systems like world-boss trigger paths.

6) Teams, chat, ping, and waypoints

  • Team lifecycle: create, join, leave, kick, lock/unlock, and request flow.
  • Dedicated team chat with quick communication format.
  • Emergency team ping with cooldown protection.
  • Shared team waypoints for regrouping and map coordination.
Open teams info page →

7) Cosmetics and titles

Visual progression includes boss auras, asteroid trails, prefixes, kill messages, and daily streak rewards.

Titles also grant passive stat bonuses. See the full title unlock/effect reference for every progression, combat, and boss title.

8) Daily rewards

Calendar and streak rewards with milestone bonuses and GUI claim flow.

9) Potions and custom effects

  • Custom potion definitions with branch-path progression.
  • Positive effects balanced by drawbacks/after-effects.
  • Custom effect IDs and status tracking support.
Open potion/effect page →

10) Custom enchants

  • Dedicated enchants for netherite tools, armor, and elytra.
  • Anvil application flow with enchant tomes.
  • Player-first guide with compatible gear and usage notes.
Open custom enchants page →

11) Custom items and recipes

Progression items include artifacts and recovery tools (for example Rebirth Stone, Totem of Life, Gracestone, and Enhanced Grindstone).

Recipe visuals are being prepared for this wiki and will be added in a later update with a Minecraft crafting-table style layout.

View custom item details →

12) Hardcore death systems

  • Revive-state handling and recovery loops.
  • Death markers and persistence windows.
  • Extra death-oriented consequences to preserve hardcore stakes.

13) Combat and mob pressure

  • Distance-based scaling to keep exploration dangerous.
  • Arrow aggro and mob behavior changes.
  • Additional combat interaction rules for survival pacing.

14) Environmental hazards

Biome-sensitive pressure profiles affect traversal and risk management (swamp, desert, mountain, and deep ocean conditions).

15) Social and UX systems

  • Inventory/EC chat sharing hooks.
  • Nickname support.
  • Tab menu overlays for live server/player info.

16) Integrations

LastBreathHC includes Discord webhook broadcasting and API-connected stat/event wiring for external service integration.
