LastBreath Plugin Documentation

This is the custom core plugin for Last Breath. It drives the Nemesis progression system, asteroid events, world bosses, and all custom RPG-style content.

Nemesis System

Persistent enemies that grow stronger, gain traits, and hunt players after major encounters.

Asteroid Events

Timed celestial impacts with high-tier loot, hostile mobs, and respawn materials.

World Bosses

Open-world raid encounters with rotating abilities and unique drops.

Custom Potions & Enchants

New combat and utility effects balanced specifically for Hardcore progression.

Custom Items

Artifact weapons, utility tools, and event items with lore and rarity tiers.

Custom Crafting Recipes

Recipe tree for progression, from survival essentials to endgame equipment.
