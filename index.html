Server Rules

Hardcore server with respawn mechanics. Griefing and alts allowed. Play smart, play hard.

1

No Hacking

Any form of cheating, X-ray, auto-clickers, or modified clients result in immediate permanent ban. We have advanced anti-cheat detection.

2

Griefing Allowed

Base raiding, stealing, and destruction are fully permitted. Protect your builds or hide them well. No rules against griefing!

3

Respect All Players

No toxicity, harassment, hate speech, or excessive spamming in chat. Keep the community friendly even while destroying each other.

4

Alt Accounts Allowed

Multiple accounts are permitted. Use alts strategically, but each must survive on their own. No ban evasion though!

5

No Exploits

Abusing glitches, dupes, or game-breaking bugs is prohibited. Report them to staff instead. This includes respawn item duping.

6

Stream Sniping

Using streams to locate and kill streamers is frowned upon. While not bannable, expect community backlash for targeted harassment.

Hardcore Death

Die and enjoy a ban... unless you have crafted a Respawn Beacon using materials from defeated asteroids. Those are rare and valuable!

Asteroid Events

Random asteroid impacts spawn tough enemies and rare materials. Defeat them to collect respawn crafting components. Risk vs reward!
