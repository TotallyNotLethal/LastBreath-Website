Combat Guide

In Hardcore, the best fighter is the player who chooses the right fight. Commit only when your exit plan is already built.

PvP survival fundamentals

  • Always open with terrain advantage: elevation, corners, and chokepoints.
  • Force shield trades only with backup food and clear spacing routes.
  • Carry two weapon options: one for burst, one for durability control.
  • Disengage early if your armor durability or hunger drops below comfort.

Mob combat in a permadeath server

  • Never chase into dark gradients; make mobs path to your light instead.
  • Torch your retreat path first, loot second.
  • Use doors, slabs, and water flow to split hostile packs.

Plugin-aware combat tips

  • Use torch carrying for safer night rotations between fight zones: Torch & Phantom Control.
  • Build fast-response roads with approved materials so your team can reinforce quickly: Path Speed Materials.
  • Run proximity callouts with Simple Voice Chat to avoid typing during active engagements.
