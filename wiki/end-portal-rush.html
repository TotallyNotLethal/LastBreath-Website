End Portal Rush

Portal rushing in Hardcore is a logistics challenge. The dragon is not the primary threat; mistakes on the way there are.

Pre-rush checklist

  • Two complete gear sets per player (active + backup).
  • Portable safety: blocks, water, pearls, and totems.
  • Coordinates for regroup points in Overworld and Nether.
  • Clear callout leader before entering stronghold sectors.

Stronghold entry discipline

  • Secure room-by-room and light every retreat route.
  • Do not cluster in portal room stairs; stagger positions.
  • Keep one player fully outside the room for overwatch.

Rush support tools

  • Build speed roads from home base to travel hubs using approved path materials: Path Speed configuration.
  • Keep late-night sky travel safer with torch carry behavior: Torch & Phantom Control.
  • Use proximity comms for crystal timing and dragon perch coordination: Simple Voice Chat setup.
