Health Management

Hardcore health is resource management. Every avoided hit is stronger than every potion consumed.

Baseline survival loadout

  • Always carry high-saturation food and one emergency instant-heal source.
  • Keep a shield on hotbar and swap totem before risky movement.
  • Manage hunger aggressively; low saturation loses long fights.

Damage prevention habits

  • Scout vertical drops and lava pockets before mining diagonals.
  • Never sprint-jump through unknown cave openings.
  • Back out of chain-damage zones (fire, fall, arrows) before countering.

Health + misc plugin synergy

  • Reduce night harassment while traveling with torch carry behavior: Torch & Phantom Control.
  • Use approved road materials to shorten dangerous travel windows: Path Speed Materials.
  • Coordinate healing calls and retreats instantly through Simple Voice Chat.
