Getting Started

Spawn, secure wood + stone tools, and get food before nightfall. Prioritize safe shelter, shields, and escape routes before committing to cave runs.

Hardcore mindset

Hardcore is a marathon, not a sprint. You do not need to rush endgame. Progress in safe steps, over-prepare, and assume every fight can go wrong.

Safety rules every player should know

  • Build your main base below Y=28. Most freecam/xray abuse will not load below this level, which makes hidden storage and bunker routes safer.
  • There is no combat log protection for PvP. If you get caught out, logging out will not save you.
  • Logging out does not despawn aggressive mobs. If something is aggroed when you leave, expect it to still be waiting when you return.
  • Learn stasis pearl teleport setups early. A prepared stasis chamber can instantly pull you out of bad positions and save runs.

Asteroids and world bosses

  • Stay away from asteroid events until you are properly geared. Treat early asteroids as high-risk zones.
  • If you spot an asteroid boss, do not rush in blind. Many players get one-shot by opening mechanics.
  • World boss portals are glowstone portals. Enter only when your team is ready with food, utility, backups, and an exit plan.

Void and death mechanics

  • Carry totems. Falling into the void with a totem can consume it, save your life, and place you back onto a block.
  • If you die in the void, revival requires a Tier 2 Revive Token.
  • Void deaths permanently wipe your ender chest contents. Plan your inventory around that risk.
