Getting Started

Welcome to Last Breath Hardcore. Your first hour matters more than your first diamond: build safe habits, play for consistency, and treat every decision as permanent.

Hardcore first-day plan (0–60 minutes)

  1. Gather wood, stone tools, and enough food to keep saturation high.
  2. Craft a shield before looking for long cave routes.
  3. Sleep or bunker before full dark unless you have full control of terrain.
  4. Set a temporary stash away from obvious travel lines.
  5. Scout base location options and commit to a defensible first bunker.

Server mechanics to learn immediately

  • Build your main storage below Y=28 for better long-term secrecy and safer routing.
  • There is no combat log protection. Logging out while threatened is not a survival tactic.
  • Aggro can persist through logouts. Re-login expecting danger, not safety.
  • Totems can save void falls, but void deaths still carry major item-loss consequences.

Use the misc plugin advantages

  • Keep a torch in your hand when traveling nights or roofless terrain to reduce phantom pressure. See the server note in Purpur Misc Extras → Torch & Phantom Control.
  • Path correctly for speed. Road systems made from approved path materials are faster to traverse. Full list: Run Faster on Path Blocks.
  • Coordinate with trusted teammates using proximity comms via Simple Voice Chat integration.

Before you leave spawn region

  • Carry: shield, blocks, water bucket, food stack, and backup pickaxe.
  • Mark fallback coordinates and a second emergency kit.
  • Do not contest events or bosses until your team has replacements for full gear loss.
