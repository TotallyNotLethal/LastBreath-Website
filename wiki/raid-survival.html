Raid Survival

When your base is hit, your objective is survival and continuity, not ego defense. Stay alive, then counter on your terms.

First 90 seconds of a raid

  • Confirm attacker count and direction before engaging.
  • Move irreplaceable kits to pre-staged dead drops.
  • Announce retreat route and regroup location immediately.
  • Prioritize line-of-sight denial (doors, smoke routes, turns).

Recovery after a forced withdrawal

  • Do not re-enter blind. Scout first with low-risk gear.
  • Rebuild logistics chain: food, arrows, armor repairs.
  • Re-establish secondary base operations before retaliation.

Raid support links
